Connecting to a Sandbox Environment?

You can connect to a Sandbox Environment by changing the login url first. Switch to the tab "Settings" and locate the textbox named "Salesforce Login Url". Enter the url of your Sandbox and click "Save" on the top right corner of the screen. Return to this screen and click on "Continue with Salesforce".
